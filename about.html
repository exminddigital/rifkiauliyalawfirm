OVERVIEW

Founder & Managing Partner

"Integrity, strategy, and a relentless commitment to justice — these are the pillars of every case we handle."

Rifki Auliya, S.H., M.H., is the Founder and Managing Partner of RA & Associates, established in 2019 and actively operating since 2023. The firm has developed into one of Indonesia's emerging law offices, focusing on litigation and strategic dispute resolution.

As an advocate, Rifki Auliya possesses extensive experience in handling significant domestic litigation matters and complex dispute cases. He has represented multinational corporations in domestic and cross-border negotiations, mediation proceedings, and court litigation. In addition, he advises national entities on administrative and commercial dispute resolution matters.

Recognized as one of the influential young litigators in Indonesia, Rifki Auliya is also known for his entrepreneurial spirit and leadership within professional and business communities.
